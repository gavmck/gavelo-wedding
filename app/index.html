Venue

We will be celebrating at Kingston Village Barn, on the outskirts of Canterbury (about 15 minutes by car from Canterbury centre).

The address is: Kingston Barn, The St, Kingston, Canterbury CT4 6JB.

Please arrive from 4pm. Drinks and food will be provided! Please see the schedule for further details.

Directions

By car from Canterbury/London: from the A2 take the exit towards Kingston/Barham/Bash 'bourne. After about 4 miles on Bonny Bush Hill, turn right onto The Street. The Barn is located on the left-hand side.

[Map] There is parking available.

Transport and Taxis

There is parking available at the Barn for those who wish to drive. There are a number of taxi companies in Canterbury

Accommodation

If you need a place to stay, we would suggest looking at the options Canterbury (which is approx. 15 minutes from Kingston Barn). There isn’t much in Kingston as it’s a tiny village!

We have negotiated a 10% discount at the Falstaff Hotel in Canterbury which we think is very nice and is where we will be staying. To book please contact the hotel directly. They are holding a limited number of rooms for us and will require payment upon booking. For the Saturday night, rates are: -Double/Twin including breakfast: 116.10. -Queen including breakfast: 94.50.

If you need further advice please ask us.
